Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

Who can submit? +
Local Philadelphia-area artists, designers, illustrators, photographers, muralists, students, and creatives. If you make things and you live, work, or hang out in the Philly area, this is for you.
What file size should I submit? +
Submit one horizontal file built at 307" wide x 145" tall.
Do I need to design a pole banner too? +
No. Submit your artwork using the horizontal specs on this page. If selected for pole banners, Color Reflections will handle the banner layout.
What is the Grand Prize? +
The Grand Prize is the massive headquarters wall mural at Color Reflections Philadelphia.
What do runners-up receive? +
Multiple runners-up may be featured on N 5th Street pole banners.
Will artists be credited? +
Yes. Winners, runners-up, and selected submissions may be featured in a dedicated website blog post and individual social media spotlights with names and Instagram tags when provided.
What file types are accepted? +
TIFF, PSD, or vector PDF preferred. High-res JPGs are accepted if print-ready.
Can I send a screenshot or social media download? +
No. Send a real print-ready file. Screenshots, 72 DPI images, social media downloads, Word documents, and low-resolution files may be rejected.
Where do I upload the artwork? +
After you fill out the submission form on this page, you will be automatically redirected to the Color Reflections WeTransfer portal at cr-philly.wetransfer.com. Upload your one horizontal file there.
What should I write in the WeTransfer message? +
Put exactly this in the WeTransfer message box: America 250 Contest - [Your Name]. For example: America 250 Contest - Jordan Smith. That is how we match your file to your form entry.
